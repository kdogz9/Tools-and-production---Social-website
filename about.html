The Origins

The Legend of the Pig

It started with a simple question in a lecture hall: "How much chaos can 100 students create?"

Greedy Piggies wasn't just a project; it was a challenge to see if a massive team could coordinate a single, polished vision. We wanted to create a game that captured the frantic, "every-pig-for-themselves" energy of university life.
